How It Works

📊 Factor Models

Each index uses a multi-factor composite score covering quality, value, safety, growth, and momentum. Factors are scored cross-sectionally (ranked against all peers) and sector-neutralized to prevent hidden concentration.

🔄 Rebalancing

The Conservative Index rebalances quarterly; the Aggressive Index monthly. Position sizing uses half-Kelly (Conservative) or equal weight (Aggressive) based on the risk profile of each strategy.

📰 Newsletter

A monthly PDF newsletter covers market conditions, index holdings, risk flags, stocks to watch, and the full methodology — published the first Sunday of each month.

🔓 Open Data

All data comes from public sources: SEC EDGAR, Yahoo Finance, and the Federal Reserve (FRED). No proprietary data feeds. All weights updated weekly.
